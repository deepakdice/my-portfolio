About Me

Solving
Problems

I'm a passionate UI/UX designer with a strong interest in solving real-world problems through design. I enjoy transforming ideas into user-friendly digital experiences.

With a background in Computer Science, I combine creativity and logic to design products that are both functional and visually appealing.

I'm constantly learning and improving my skills in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and interaction design.
